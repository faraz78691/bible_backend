PERSONAL DATA WE COLLECT

When using the Xder Platforms, we collect Personal Data about you, most of which you provide voluntarily. We may also obtain data about your interactions, usage and experiences on our Platforms, as well as receive information from third parties.

Personal Data You Must Provide: When creating an account, you are required to provide a limited amount of Personal Data, such as an email address or phone number, along with your date of birth to verify that you are of legal age.

Personal Data You May Provide: You are free to decide what Personal Data you wish to include in your “Xder Profile”. This Personal Profile Data, known as “Profile Information”, is shared with other users of the Xder Services, i.e. the “Xder Community”. This includes options like sharing your location, photos and videos. However, please note that omitting certain data could affect the functionality of some Xder Services. You can also choose whether to provide Personal Data to provide feedback, use customer services, or participate in surveys. The types of data collected may vary depending on the survey.

Note on Sensitive Personal Data: In some jurisdictions and under certain regulations, Personal Data that may reveal a person’s racial or ethnic origin may be considered “sensitive” or “special category” Personal Data.

Notice About Location Information: We may collect your exact location (“Location”) to determine your distance from other users (“Distance Data”). Advanced users who use the Xder Services in an unauthorized manner or who change locations while you remain there may use Distance Information to determine your location and possibly your identity. You can choose to hide your distance information, but the Xder Services will still sort and display your profile based on your relative distance from other users. Even if you choose to hide this information, other people may still be able to determine your location. Additionally, you have the option to revoke this permission and disable location services on your device. On iPhone, you can do this from Settings > Privacy > Location Services > Xder, and on Android devices, from Settings > Location > Xder > Location Permissions, adjusting your browser settings.

Personal Data We Receive from Your Device When Using the Xder Platforms: In order to maintain and improve the Xder Platforms, we collect Personal Data such as user activity, hardware and software information, cookies and use other technologies, such as web beacons, software development kits (SDKs), local storage and log files.
